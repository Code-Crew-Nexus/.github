# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

CODE CREW NEXUS is intended to be a respectful, collaborative, and learning-focused organization.

All contributors, maintainers, reviewers, and collaborators are expected to help create an environment that is:

- welcoming,
- respectful,
- constructive,
- and professional.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in communication.
- Give feedback that is clear and constructive.
- Support learning and collaboration.
- Respect differences in skill level, experience, and background.
- Focus discussions on improving the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or hostile behavior.
- Personal attacks or mockery.
- Disrespectful language.
- Deliberately obstructing collaboration.
- Misrepresenting someone else's work as your own.

## Academic Integrity

Since this organization may include university and student work:

- do not submit misleading or dishonest contributions,
- do not claim authorship for work you did not do,
- and do not bypass project expectations unfairly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and discussions that do not align with this code of conduct.

Repeated or serious violations may result in restricted collaboration access.

## Contact

If something needs to be reported, contact the repository maintainers or organization owner privately.
